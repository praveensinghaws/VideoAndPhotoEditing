JustPopInOut - Davinci Resolve 17 timeline effect

Effect created by William Justice | https://billjustice.com

The JustPopInOut effect will take any text/clip and zoom in from 0 to 1 at the beginning of the clip and zoom out from 1 to 0 at the end of the clip.  The timeline automatically adjusts to the length of the clip.

INSTALL
  - Download effect files
  - UnZip effect
  - Copy the files to the folder location


FILE LOCATIONS
PC
/Users/%username%/AppData/Roaming/Blackmagic Design/DaVinci Resolve/Support/Fusion/Templates/Edit/Effects/

MAC
/Library/Application Support/Blackmagic Design/DaVinci Resolve/Fusion/Templates/Edit/Effects/


FIND LOCATIONS IN RESOLVE
  - In Resolve Click the Fusion button
  - Open the effects panel
  - Click Edit Templates and then Effects
  - In the top right area, click "..." and choose Show Folder
